About Our Journey

Puppet House is Bahrain first specialized organization dedicated to children's theater. operating as a dynamic online platform through Instagram and Youtube. we identify ourselves as "traveling Theater", bringing our creative performances and workshops directly to children in schools, festivals, and events across the Kingdom. it is a creative sanctuary where children find their own space to explore and understand life through their unique perspective.

Established in 2019 by the renowned puppet creator Ahmed Jassim, our ultimate vision is to become a global ambassador for this timeless art. we aim to expand globally , taking our shows to international stages and touching the hearts of children and parents worldwide. Puppet House is more then just entertaiment, we strive to spread the culture of puppetry in our communities. We foster imagination and take children on a complete creative journey, from the first sparkle on an idea to the final production of a show.

About the Founder

Read more about the founder

born in 1972 in the Kingdom of Bahrain, Ahmed Jassim's journey began in a classroom as an Arabic teacher. His passion for storytelling led him from teaching letters to cteating magical worls. with over 20 ywars of experience, he has become a poineer in puppetary, blending vocal performence, acting, and master crafsmanship.

To perfect his art, Ahmed traveled to Tunisia, France, Qatar and many more cuntries, learning from the best puppet masters. from his first show "win Al watan" in 2006 to his influential work on Bahrain TV's "Talawen", he has dedicated his life to this rare art. For Ahmed, every face is a potentile puppet, and every puppet carry a massage of education and imagination to children everywhere.
